# Getting Started

This guide walks you through setting up AckAgent on your computer and mobile device.

## Overview

Setting up AckAgent involves three steps:

1. **Install the mobile app** on your iPhone or Android device & create your account
2. **Install the CLI** on your computer (macOS or Linux)
3. **Log in and pair** your devices with SAS verification

After setup, you can generate signing keys and configure integrations for Git, SSH, Claude Code, or Age encryption.

## Requirements

**Computer:**

- macOS 12+ (Intel or Apple Silicon)
- Linux (x86_64 or ARM64)

**Mobile Device:**

- iOS 18.6+ (iPhone with Secure Enclave)
- Android 9+ (device with StrongBox or TEE)
